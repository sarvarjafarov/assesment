How it works

Evalon runs each search in four simple steps.

  1. 1

    Intake

    Plan & scope

    Pick a catalog assessment or clone your own rubric. Set deadlines, reviewers, and notes in minutes.

  2. 2

    Invite

    Invite talent

    Evalon emails every candidate a friendly briefing page with rules, a timeline, and the start link.

  3. 3

    Score

    Auto score & review

    Evalon scores every response instantly. Recruiters see benchmarks, notes, and audit trails.

  4. 4

    Decide

    Advance with confidence

    Push finalists to interviews or offers and keep candidates informed automatically.
