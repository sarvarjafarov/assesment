How it works

From request to decision in four steps.

  1. 1

    Intake & scope

    Choose a catalog assessment or clone your own rubric. Add deadlines, reviewers, and notes.

  2. 2

    Invite talent

    Sira emails every candidate a personalized briefing page with rules, timeline, and start link.

  3. 3

    Auto score & review

    Responses are scored instantly. Hiring teams see benchmarks, notes, and audit trails.

  4. 4

    Advance with confidence

    Push shortlisted talent to interviews or offers and keep candidates informed.
