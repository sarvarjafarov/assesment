Jobs

Create and manage positions, organize them into hiring campaigns

{# ── Positions ── #}
Positions

Each position represents a role you're hiring for. The Jobs page uses two tabs: Positions (individual roles) and Campaigns (groups of related positions).{% if is_manager %} As a manager, you can create, edit, and clone positions.{% endif %}

When creating a position, you define:

Title & description — the role name and a detailed job description that candidates see on your vacancy page
Department & location — used for organizing and filtering positions (e.g., "Engineering" in "New York")
Employment type — full-time, part-time, contract, internship, or freelance
Work model — on-site, remote, or hybrid
Salary range — minimum and maximum salary with currency, shown to applicants
Required skills — key skills for the role, used for matching and display
Responsibilities, requirements & nice-to-haves — structured lists displayed on the vacancy page for candidates
Assessment type — which assessment candidates should complete (candidates who apply via your vacancy page receive it automatically)
Open roles & deadline — how many hires you need and when the position closes (auto-archives after deadline)
Priority level — P0 (critical) through P3 (low), helps you sort and focus on urgent hires first
Published status — toggle visibility on your public vacancy page. Only published + active positions appear to candidates
{% if can_use_ai_hiring %}
AI Screening Pro — toggle to enable automatic resume scoring and candidate pipeline automation
{% endif %}
{# ── Position Detail ── #}
Position Detail & Pipeline

Click any position to see its detail page with a full overview and candidate pipeline:

Position health — total candidates, completion rate, active invites, and score distribution at a glance
Pipeline kanban — visual board showing candidates across stages: Invited → In Progress → Submitted → Reviewed → Shortlisted / Rejected
Candidate cards — each card shows name, score, assessment type, and level. {% if is_manager %}Drag-and-drop between stages to update status{% endif %}
Clone position — duplicate any position to quickly create similar roles with the same settings
{# ── Campaigns ── #}
Campaigns

Group related positions into a hiring campaign (e.g., "Q1 Engineering Hiring" or "Summer Internship Program"). Campaigns let you:

Organize by project — keep related roles together under one umbrella
Track aggregate stats — see total positions and active count per campaign
Manage status — active, paused, completed, or archived

AI Screening Pro

Automate the first stage of your hiring pipeline with AI-powered resume screening

1

Enable on a Position

Edit any position and toggle "Enable AI Screening." Choose your automation level based on how much control you want:

Screen only Screen + auto-send Full automation

You also set a screening threshold (minimum AI score to pass, default 60) and a passing score (minimum assessment score to advance).

2

Candidates Get Scored Automatically

When a candidate applies through your vacancy page, their resume is automatically analyzed against the job description. The AI evaluates:

Resume score (0–100) — overall fit for the role
Matched skills — skills found in the resume that match your requirements
Missing skills — required skills not found in the resume
AI recommendation — a written summary of the candidate's strengths and weaknesses

Candidates scoring at or above your threshold (default 60) are marked "passed" and advance to the next stage based on your automation mode.

3

Review & Make Decisions

The Candidates page shows an "AI Score" column so you can quickly identify top candidates. On each candidate's detail page:

View AI analysis — full screening results, matched/missing skills, and recommendation
Submit human review — advance, hold, or reject with your own notes. Your decision overrides the AI
Processing log — a full timeline of every AI and human action taken on the candidate
Candidate Pipeline Stages

AI-screened candidates move through these stages automatically (or manually, depending on your automation mode):

Uploaded → Screening → Shortlisted → Assessment Sent → Completed → Decision
{% else %}

AI Screening Pro

Available on Pro & Enterprise plans

AI Screening automatically analyzes candidate resumes, scores them against your job requirements, and advances top candidates through your pipeline — saving hours of manual resume review.

Automatic resume scoring (0–100) against your job description
Skill matching — see which required skills candidates have or lack
Three automation modes — from screen-only to fully automated pipeline
Human review override — you always have final say
Upgrade to Pro →

Reports & Analytics

Data-driven insights into your hiring performance

Key metrics tracked:

Total invites sent — how many assessment invites you've sent across all types
Completion rate — percentage of invited candidates who actually submitted their assessment
In-progress count — candidates currently taking an assessment
Average score — mean overall score across all completed assessments
Average duration — typical time from assessment start to submission
Breakdowns & Distributions
Assessment breakdown — per-type stats (invites, completed, rate)
Score distribution — histogram showing how candidates score (0–40, 40–60, 60–80, 80–100)
Hiring funnel — invited → started → completed drop-off analysis
Trends & Filters
Time period filters — last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or all time
Trend analysis — completion rate and score trends over time
CSV export — download all session data for external analysis
{# ═══════════════════════════════════════════ #} {# ── 10. Notifications ── #} {# ═══════════════════════════════════════════ #}

Notifications

Stay informed about candidate activity and system events

Evalon sends real-time notifications so you never miss important hiring events. Access your notifications from the bell icon in the sidebar.

Notification types:

Assessment completed — a candidate finished and submitted their assessment
Candidate started — a candidate began taking their assessment
Scoring complete — an assessment has been auto-scored and results are ready
{% if can_use_ai_hiring %}
Pipeline update Pro — AI screening progress or stage changes
Decision recorded Pro — a human review decision was submitted
{% endif %}
System notifications — plan updates, security alerts, and platform announcements

Each notification links directly to the relevant page. You can mark individual notifications as read or use "Mark all read" to clear them. Configure which notifications you receive via email in Settings.
